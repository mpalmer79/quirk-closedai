Goals & KPIs

Speed-to-lead:
< 10 min (email + SMS)
Booked appts:
≥ 30% of qual. leads
No-shows:
< 20%

Cadence (Day 0–7)

Day 0: Email + SMS + Call (voicemail if no answer)
Day 1: Email + SMS
Day 2: Call + SMS
Day 3: Email
Day 5: Call
Day 7: Final closeout (re-open on response)
